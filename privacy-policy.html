The Gathering Conference Center ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard the information you provide through our website, located at thegathering365.com, event inquiry forms, and other digital interactions.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, request pricing, check venue availability, or schedule a tour, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This information includes:

  • Contact Details: First and last name, email address, physical address, and telephone number.
  • Event Specifications: Category of event (wedding, banquet, corporate seminar, social party), preferred event date and times, estimated guest counts, and special accommodation requests.
  • Message Details: Text and inquiries entered directly into our contact forms.

We also automatically collect standard, non-identifying technical metadata through our website hosting providers, cookies, and analytic services, such as your IP address, browser type, device details, and navigation path on our website.

2. How We Use Information

We process the personal details you submit to operate our business activities, specifically to:

  • Evaluate, schedule, and confirm your event venue requests.
  • Email customized rental quotes, configurations, catering menus, and brochures.
  • Follow up on your inquiry via phone calls, text messages, or emails.
  • Analyze web traffic to fix display layout rendering and optimize user experience.

3. Phone, Email, and SMS Communications

By submitting an inquiry and providing your phone number on our booking form, you consent to receive text messages (SMS) and telephone calls from The Gathering Conference Center regarding venue booking dates, rates, availability, or planning details.

Message and data rates may apply. SMS message frequency varies depending on your interactive inquiries. You may opt out of future text messages at any time by replying STOP. For further support, reply HELP or email us at info@thegathering365.com.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing campaigns. We share information only with trusted service providers who perform business operations on our behalf (such as mapping services, web hosting platforms, email clients, text messaging gateways, and customer support databases). These vendors are restricted from using your data for any purpose other than providing these specific services.

5. Cookies and Third-Party Tracking

We use essential and functional cookies to remember layout choices and facilitate form submissions. We also embed third-party components (such as Google Maps) which may collect browser identifier and usage metadata under their own respect privacy terms. You can decline cookie collection through our cookie consent banner or by adjusting browser settings, though doing so might disable certain interactive features.

6. Data Security and Retention

We use administrative and technical safeguards (such as HTTPS/SSL encryption) designed to protect details submitted via forms against unauthorized access, loss, or manipulation. However, no data transmitting system over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

We retain inquiry details as long as needed to satisfy business planning, customer service communication, reporting, and legal recordkeeping rules.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA Compliant)

If you are a resident of California, you have the right to request details about the categories of personal details we collect, the sources of collection, and our business purposes for processing. You also possess the right to request deletion or correction of your personal data. To submit a California privacy request, please contact us using the details below. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliant)

Our services and website are structured exclusively for businesses and adults planning events. We do not design our pages for, nor do we knowingly collect details from, children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted information to us, please notify us immediately so we can remove the data from our active storage systems.
