The Gathering Conference Center is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We are continuously improving our digital systems to make our website, venue services, and booking widgets user-friendly for all visitors.

1. Our Commitment

We strive to provide a website experience that is usable by individuals utilizing assistive technologies, including screen readers, keyboard navigation controls, text magnification tools, and speech recognition software. Our goal is to ensure equal opportunity and digital inclusion for all community members in Tupelo and beyond.

2. Standards and Conformance Level

To evaluate accessibility, we align our web platform development with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA conformance standards. These guidelines describe best practices for making content perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for a wide range of people with physical, sensory, or cognitive differences.

3. Key Accessibility Features

We have integrated specific accessibility features across our website structure, including:

  • Skip to Content Link: A keyboard-accessible skip link is present at the top of every page, allowing visitors using screen readers or keyboard navigations to bypass main menu tabs and jump directly to the primary page contents.
  • Alt Text Descriptions: All informational images, photo galleries, and configurations include alternative text parameters describing the image subject matter for screen readers.
  • Semantic Structure: Section boundaries are wrapped in HTML5 structural landmarks (such as <header>, <main>, and <footer>) to allow screen reader users to browse using headings.
  • Keyboard Control: All forms, sliders, tabs, and buttons are designed to allow keyboard focus outlines and interactive selection via the Tab and Enter/Space keys.
  • Contrast Ratios: Text elements are styled to meet or exceed contrast standards of 4.5:1 against their backgrounds for maximum readability.

4. Third-Party Exclusions

Please note that our website incorporates embedded components and widgets hosted by third-party platforms (for example, Google Maps for interactive directions). Because these scripts are maintained externally, some features or markup may not fully align with our conformance goals. We continue to advocate for accessibility updates from our digital vendors.

5. Ongoing Improvements

We perform ongoing checks, visual evaluations, and keyboard navigation test sweeps to isolate and fix potential access barriers. Our team is trained on accessibility guidelines, and we review digital updates to ensure they meet our standards.
