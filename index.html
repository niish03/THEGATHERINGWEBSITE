About The Gathering

Designed for comfort, connection, and memorable gatherings.

Nestled in the heart of Tupelo, Mississippi, The Gathering Conference Center is where Southern charm meets modern elegance. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate meeting, conference, private celebration, or community event, our beautifully designed venue provides the perfect setting for unforgettable occasions.

With flexible event spaces, upscale amenities, and a dedicated team committed to exceptional service, we make it easy to bring your vision to life. From intimate gatherings to large celebrations, we're here to ensure every detail is thoughtfully planned and every guest feels welcome.

Our Venue Spaces

Our venue includes four spacious rooms separated by sliding sound-resistant partition dividers. Rooms may be rented individually or combined depending on event size, layout, and availability.

Room A - Boardroom Setup

Room A

📏 Dimensions: 40 × 60 ft ✨ Capacity: Up to 60 Guests
Square Footage
2,400 sq. ft.
Ideal Layouts
Classroom, Boardroom, Banquet

Great for meetings, private gatherings, and smaller events. Room A offers an intimate, highly focused setting equipped with direct access to power terminals.

Sound-Reducing Sliding Dividers
Separated by acoustic panel dividers to guarantee privacy from neighboring events. Slide them open to merge with Room B.

Room B

📏 Dimensions: 62 × 60 ft ✨ Capacity: Up to 110 Guests
Square Footage
3,720 sq. ft.
Ideal Layouts
Theater, Reception, Banquet

A spacious option for larger setups and special occasions. Room B provides a grand feel, making it the perfect setting for upscale receptions and social dinners.

Need More Space?
Combine Room B with Rooms A and C by sliding back the sound-reducing partition walls to host larger dinner parties.

Room C

Square Footage

Ideal Layouts
Training Seminars, Small Showers

Ideal for focused events, trainings, and celebrations. Room C offers a cozy, highly functional space for baby showers, training seminars, or meetings.

Modular Options
Operates independently or easily integrates with adjacent rooms for buffet line setups.

Room D

📏 Dimensions: 55 × 60 ft ✨ Capacity: Up to 80 Guests

Banquet, Cocktail Party, Dance

Flexible space for banquets, parties, and community events. Room D features high power capacity and an layout adaptable for dance floors and music.
